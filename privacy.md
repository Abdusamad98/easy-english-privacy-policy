## Information Collection and Use
English Lug'atim is designed to work without an account. The core features —
the offline English↔Uzbek dictionary, English word definitions, and your
personal "My Words" vocabulary list — run entirely on your device and do **not**
require you to provide any personal information.

We collect the following information only in specific, optional situations:

- **Account information (optional).** Signing in is never required. If you
  choose to create an account or sign in using Google, Apple, or
  email/password, we receive your email address and, where the provider makes
  it available, your display name and profile picture. We do **not** collect
  your gender, physical address, or precise location.
- **Community content.** If you post a message on the Community comments wall,
  the text of your message and the date it was posted are stored and made
  publicly visible to other users of the app.
- **Microphone (optional).** The voice-search feature uses your device's
  microphone and speech-recognition service to convert your speech into search
  text. The microphone is only accessed while you actively use voice search,
  and we do not store audio recordings.
- **Push notifications.** To deliver news and updates, your device is registered
  for push notifications and may be assigned a notification token.

The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in
this privacy policy. The app does use third-party services that may collect
information used to identify you.
